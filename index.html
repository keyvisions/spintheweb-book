The Spin the Web Foundation stewards the open-source Spin the Web framework, designed for building enterprise web portals. Our mission is to virtualize the enterprise brand (eBranding) by unifying diverse systems like ERP, CRM, and BPMS into a single, consistent digital channel for all stakeholders.
